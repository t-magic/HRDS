## notebook名を変更するには

When you open your notebook, you should see an "I" "Notebook Info" icon (see below)

 you have to be in edit mode first. Then click on the "I" icon.  

![](pict/WS000015.JPG)

There you can click on the Name field to change the notebook name.

![](pict/WS000011.JPG)

![](pict/WS000021.JPG)
